Banking Choices in Bhutan 📊

Insights from 111 respondents

Introduction

Banking is evolving in Bhutan with increasing digital adoption.

  • Understand banking behavior
  • Identify gaps
  • Provide solutions

Findings

Bank of Bhutan dominates preferences.

Trust and service quality are key decision factors.

Solutions

  • Improve digital banking
  • Enhance accessibility
  • Financial literacy programs

Conclusion

Improving trust, service, and accessibility will increase satisfaction.
