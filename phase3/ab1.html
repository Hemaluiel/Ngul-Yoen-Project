Customer Banking Choices in Bhutan 🇧🇹

About Ngul~Yoen

Ngul Yoen is a web-based platform providing insights into banking choices in Bhutan, helping users make informed financial decisions.

About the Project

This project analyzes customer banking choices and key factors like trust, service quality, accessibility, and ease of use.

It aims to identify improvement areas for banks and empower customers with better decision-making.
