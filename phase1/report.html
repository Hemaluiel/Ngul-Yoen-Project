Customer Banking Choices in Bhutan

Introduction

Banking services play an important role in people’s daily lives. With the growth of digital banking, the financial landscape of Bhutan is rapidly changing. Banking services are now accessible across the country in both traditional and digital forms.

Problem Statement

Despite the importance of banking services, customers experience varying levels of satisfaction. Issues exist in trust, service quality, accessibility, and ease of use.

Main Motives

  • Understand customer banking choices
  • Identify customer issues
  • Provide practical solutions

Research Questions

  • What are the patterns of customer banking choices in Bhutan?
  • What areas need improvement in banking services?

Significance of the Study

This study helps banks understand customer preferences and improve services in terms of trust, service quality, accessibility, and ease of use.

Literature Review

Previous studies highlight trust, service quality, accessibility, and ease of use as key factors influencing banking choices. However, limited research exists on customer challenges and solutions in Bhutan.

Methodology

A mixed-method approach was used with data collected from 111 respondents through surveys. Tools used include Google Forms, Google Sheets, Tableau, and web platforms.

Findings and Analysis

Overview

Most respondents have bank accounts, but some remain unbanked due to lack of awareness or income.

Customer Banking Choices

The majority (84.47%) prefer Bank of Bhutan, showing strong dominance in the market.

Factor: Trust

Over 80% of respondents trust their bank, though some concerns remain regarding security and communication.

Factor: Service Quality

Most customers are satisfied, but a small percentage highlight service gaps.

Factor: Accessibility

Accessibility varies by location, with rural areas facing more challenges.

Factor: Ease of Use

Customers value simple and flexible services, though network issues affect usability.

Future Insight

Many customers are unsure about switching banks, indicating opportunities for improvement and competition.

Discussion and Solutions

1. Banking Assistance Website

A web platform can guide users, compare banks, and improve financial literacy.

2. Suggestions for Bank of Bhutan

  • Improve internet banking services
  • Add budgeting features in mobile banking

Conclusion

Customer banking choices in Bhutan are influenced by trust, service quality, accessibility, and ease of use. Addressing these factors can improve customer satisfaction and loyalty.
