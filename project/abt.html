About Ngul~Yoen

The platform and its purpose

Ngul Yoen is a web-based solution platform dedicated to surfacing insights about banking choices in Bhutan and act as the solution to customer banking chalenges. The name blends the Dzongkha word for money with scope — reflecting how financial decisions and insights shape everyday life in the kingdom.

The platform aims to bridge the gap between raw survey data and actionable understanding, giving both customers and institutions a clearer picture of what drives banking behaviour in Bhutan today.

About the project

story, scope & objectives

This project analyses what drives Bhutanese banking customers when choosing a bank. Four core factors were studied: trust, service quality, accessibility, and ease of use — each explored through a structured survey designed to capture genuine customer sentiment.

The project aims to highlight where banks can improve and to empower customers with better information for their own financial decisions through a platform that educate people on banking insights and solution to banking challenges. Findings are presented through charts, written analysis, and interactive tools.

My role in Bhutan DataScientists was the role of data practitioner - a professional who manage, analyze, and leaverage data to solve real world problems.
