About FinSight

What it is, who it's for, and why it was built

FinSight is an AI-powered bank statement analyzer designed to take the manual work out of understanding your finances. Instead of scrolling through dozens of transactions and guessing where your money went, you simply upload your bank statement PDF and let FinSight do the work.

Built with Bhutanese banking in mind — and tested on real BoB and DK Bank e-statements — FinSight recognises local transaction formats, merchant names, and payment patterns. The AI engine then maps every debit to a meaningful expense category automatically.

🎓

Built for students

Designed with GCBS students in mind — track SEMSO contributions, food expenses, and personal spending without complicated spreadsheets.

🏢

Useful for everyone

Anyone with a Bhutanese bank account can upload their monthly e-statement and get a clear financial picture instantly.

🔒

Session-based privacy

Your statement is processed for the current session only. No data is stored permanently — your financial details stay yours.

🇧🇹

Bhutan-first design

Supports BTN (Ngultrum) currency, local bank formats (BoB, DK Bank, BNB), and common Bhutanese merchant categories.

⚙️

How it works

Three simple steps from upload to insight

1

Upload your bank statement

Download your e-statement PDF from your bank's internet banking portal (BoB, BNB, BDBL, DK Bank, T Bank, or Druk PNB all work). Then drag and drop — or click to upload — directly into FinSight. No account creation required.

📄 PDF upload · No sign-up needed
2

AI reads and categorises every transaction

The moment your statement uploads, FinSight's AI engine parses every transaction line — debits, credits, dates, merchant names — and automatically assigns each debit to an expense category. Categories include Shopping, Transfers, Food & Drinks, Utilities, Transport, Education, and more. The AI learns from transaction descriptions and patterns to make categorisation accurate even for unusual entries.

🤖 AI-powered · Instant categorization · 16+ category types
3

Get your full financial snapshot

Your results appear immediately — total debits and credits, number of spending categories found, biggest spending category, top 5 transactions by amount, a spending breakdown donut chart, and a plain-English AI insight summarising your month's financial behaviour.

📊 Dashboard · Charts · AI insight · Top transactions

AI insight — plain English analysis

Not just numbers — a narrative explanation of your spending behaviour

Beyond charts and totals, FinSight uses AI to write a short, plain-English summary of your statement. It explains the patterns it found — your biggest spending areas, notable transactions, and overall financial behaviour for the period — in a way that's easy to understand without being a finance expert.

💡
AI Insight — example from Feb 2026

"The majority of the expenses are transfer related, with a significant amount of 4,000 transferred on FEB 19. There are also some shopping and utility expenses, with a total of 2,100 spent on shopping. The credit balance carried forward is 1,242.67."

Understand your spending story without reading every line of a statement

Identify which categories dominate your expenses month to month
See your credit balance carried forward clearly stated in plain language
