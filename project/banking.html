🏦 Bhutan Banking Insights Dashboard

Key information about major banks in Bhutan

Bank of Bhutan (BoB)
Established: 1968
• Oldest bank in Bhutan
• Main purpose: Retail + corporate banking
• Branches: ~47+
• ATMs: Nationwide coverage
• Services: Loans, savings, forex, cards
Bhutan National Bank (BNB)
Established: 1997
• Second largest bank
• Main purpose: Digital + retail banking
• Branches: ~30+
• ATMs: Strong network
• Services: mPay, loans, cards, remittance
T Bank
Established: 2010s
• Private commercial bank
• Main purpose: Digital-first banking
• Branches: Limited physical branches
• ATMs: Growing network
• Services: TPay, T Wallet, remittance
Bhutan Development Bank (BDBL)
Established: 1988 (merged)
• Focus on rural development
• Main purpose: Agriculture + SME financing
• Branches: Wide rural coverage
• ATMs: Limited
• Services: Microfinance, rural loans
Druk PNB Bank
Established: 2010
• Joint venture bank
• Main purpose: Commercial + retail banking
• Branches: Moderate network
• ATMs: Urban focused
• Services: Loans, deposits, trade finance
Royal Monetary Authority (RMA)
Central Bank
• Bhutan’s central bank
• Main purpose: Monetary policy & regulation
• Branches: Not retail bank
• ATMs: N/A
• Services: Currency control, banking supervision
