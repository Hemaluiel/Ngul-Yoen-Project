Customer Banking Choices

Kuzuzangpo la! Explore key insights on banking choices in Bhutan 🇧🇹

📌 Key Findings

  1. Most respondents (103 out of 111) have bank accounts, but a small group remains unbanked due to lack of awareness, income, or age limitations.
  2. The Bank of Bhutan (BOB) is the most preferred bank, with about 84% of respondents choosing it.
  3. Key factors influencing bank choice include trust, service quality, accessibility, and ease of use. Trust is a major factor, with over 80% of customers expressing confidence in their bank.
  4. Service quality is generally good, but around 7–10% of users reported dissatisfaction.
  5. Accessibility varies by location: rural users face limited access, while urban users experience congestion.
  6. Ease of use of digital banking is positive overall, but network issues affect some users.
  7. Many customers are uncertain or willing to switch banks, showing opportunities for improvement.
  8. There is a clear gap in financial literacy and awareness.
