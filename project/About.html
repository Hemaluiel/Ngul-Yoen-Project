About Ngul~Yoen

Ngul Yoen is the project website aimed at providing insights into the banking choices in Bhutan, helps people choose the right bank for them and make informed decisions about their banking needs.

About Customer Banking Choices

This proejct gives a deeper insights into customer banking choices and the factors affecting those choices (such as trust, satisfaction, service quality, accessibility and ease of use). It also focus solution driven and result oriented outcome.

The main aim of the project is to get clear insight into customer banking choice and patterns, and helping bank idenify the areas for improvement and the opportunities for incorporating additional services. So that the customers can make informed decisions about their banking needs.

Moreover, the project also aims to provide insights into the banking sector in Bhutan, and help people make informed decisions about their banking needs as the solution to the current customer challenges through this web-based platform.

About Me

I am Hema Luitel, a third-year student at Gedu College of Business Studies, pursuing a Bachelor of Business Intelligence with a major in Financial Engineering.

This work is a part of the six-month internship at Bhutan Data Scientists Pvt. Ltd. as a data practitioner.

About Bhutan Data Scientists Pvt. Ltd.
